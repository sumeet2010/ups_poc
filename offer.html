Activity Oriented Learning

We try to make children inquisitive by nature through healthy questionnaire and practical demonstration sessions. Morning assembly is an essential part of our curricula. Debate and quiz competitions are organized regularly throughout the session.

Integrated Learning

Udai public school has a healthy, dynamic and conducive environment for teaching learning process facilitated by highly competent and well-experienced teaching staff who apply modern means, techniques and skills of teaching. Both the teachers and the students are enabled to enjoy their work, even the complex and complicated ones with sincere care and effort. The children are given opportunities to let their expressions have exposure to real life situations and their innate capacity is developed through co-curricular activities viz. SUPW, debates, seminars, dramatics, quizzes etc. to strengthen their mental faculty. Emphasis is also laid on the development of analytical thinking and constructive vision of the world as a whole.

Computer Lab

Beginning from Class first onwards, Computer Education is an integral part of the school curriculum. The students are trained in basic skills and techniques of computer. Sports

We have fabulous facilities for sports and games. Our students take part in inter-house sports competitions.

Co-curricular Activities

Students of our school are exposed to various co-curricular activities like debate, declamation, group / solo songs, dance, dramatics, essay writing, spelling and quiz competitions, Hindi pakhwada etc.

Personality Development

We lay stress on the personality development.. We also invite experts to deliver talks on personality building. Guidance and counseling programs are also organized frequently.

Personal File

The school maintains an individual file for all its students to have a systematic record for the academic as well as co-curricular progress and achievements.

Identity

The child's identity page in the diary bearing a passport size photograph and the address of the student will remain in his/her custody all the time.

Private Tuition

Private Tuition as a rule is discouraged as they sap the initiative of students. Parents are requested not to approach the school teachers for tuition.
